Our Story

Built on Vision.
Driven by Results.

Avantec Media was founded in 2001 with a single conviction: that great visual storytelling changes outcomes. For nearly a decade, we built digital presences for companies across industries — learning that what truly moves people is not a clever layout, but a compelling image.

After a decade away from the industry, we're back — and we've traded pixels for lenses. Today, Avantec Media brings that same obsession with craft and results to real estate photography, videography, and drone cinematography in the Tampa Bay area and beyond.

Whether it's a starter home or a waterfront estate, we approach every shoot the same way: with precision, intention, and an eye for the detail that makes a buyer stop scrolling.
